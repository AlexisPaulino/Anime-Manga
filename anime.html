Anime Reviews

Made in Abyss
  • Author: Akihito Tsukushi
  • Status: Ongoing
  • Genre: Adventure, Fantasy, Drama
  • Personal Rating: 8/10

A young girl, Riko, dreams of finding her mother, who disappeared into the Abyss. Accompanied by a robot boy, Riko journeys deep into the Abyss, uncovering secrets and facing dangers. This anime had a lot of wtf moments but it really pulls at the heart strings.

Eden of the East
  • Author: Kenji Kamiyama
  • Status: Completed
  • Genre: Thriller, Mystery, Political
  • Personal Rating: 8/10

A young man with amnesia and a mysterious power is thrust into a complex game involving ten billion yen and a group of individuals trying to save Japan from disaster. Eden of the East was such a blast to watch it gave a lot of studio ghibli vibes.

Re:Zero
  • Author: Tappei Nagatsuki
  • Status: Ongoing
  • Genre: Psychological, Drama, Fantasy
  • Personal Rating: 20/10

Subaru is transported to another world, where he discovers he has the ability to rewind time upon death. The story is a dark and emotional journey of suffering, growth, and perseverance. Some arcs are brutal but so rewarding when you stick with it. Re:Zero has become my second most favorite anime of all time the story is PEAK.

Hunter x Hunter
  • Author: Yoshihiro Togashi
  • Status: Ongoing (hiatus)
  • Genre: Adventure, Fantasy, Action
  • Personal Rating: 10/10

Gon Freecss embarks on a journey to find his missing father, meeting friends and facing deadly challenges as a Hunter. This anime has some of the best story arcs and emotional development in shonen history. My fourth favorite anime of all time hunter x hunter's story and power system is really amazing.
