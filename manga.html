Manga Reviews

Soul Eater
  • Author: Atsushi Ōkubo
  • Status: Completed
  • Genre: Action, Supernatural, Dark Fantasy
  • Personal Rating: 10/10

Students at Death Weapon Meister Academy battle evil souls to protect the world. It's stylish, chaotic, and has one of the coolest art styles I've seen in a manga. Soul Eater is my fifth favorite of all time the power system, characters, and music are all amazing!

The Promised Neverland
  • Author: Kaiu Shirai
  • Status: Completed
  • Genre: Horror, Thriller, Science Fiction
  • Personal Rating: 9/10

At first, it seems like a perfect orphanage, but when the truth is uncovered, the kids must escape a horrifying fate. The twists and mind games are insane. The Goldy Pond arc will have you on an emotional roller coaster!

Black Clover
  • Author: Yūki Tabata
  • Status: Ongoing
  • Genre: Action, Fantasy, Adventure
  • Personal Rating: 10/10

In a world where magic is everything, Asta is a boy born without any. He strives to become the Wizard King. Classic underdog story with nonstop hype fights. Black clover will always have a special place in my heart I was a big fan even during its days where everyone was hating on it. I never seen a story that makes every single character main or side shine as bright as this one!

Shangri-La Frontier
  • Author: Katarina
  • Status: Ongoing
  • Genre: Adventure, Fantasy, Sci-Fi
  • Personal Rating: 10/10

A "trash game hunter" tries out a god-tier VRMMO game and ends up taking on insane challenges. If you love gaming stories, this one is really refreshing and funny. This is my third all time favorite anime/manga series this game has inspired me to pursue game development!

Jujutsu Kaisen
  • Author: Gege Akutami
  • Status: Completed
  • Genre: Action, Supernatural, Dark Fantasy
  • Personal Rating: 9/10

Yuji Itadori gets dragged into a world of curses after swallowing a powerful cursed object. The fights are really amazing! A lot of deep lore, and painful emotional moments everywhere.
